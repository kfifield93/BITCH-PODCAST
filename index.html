About the Podcast

Hey, Canadians! Do you know your rights? Or are you more familiar with the rights of Americans?

Media and pop culture may give us the impression that we know the laws and rights that regulate the world around us, but much of what we know about Canadian laws might be assumptions influenced by what we see from the US.

Join Karen and Alex as they sort through Canadian laws, regulations, and rights, reviewing real Canadian cases that reference or even influence the law in question.

We’re not Un-American, we’re just not American.

About the Hosts

Karen Silver is a children's author and journalist from St. John's, NL. With a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University of Newfoundland, she’s passionate about keeping Canadians informed about their rights and laws. As the creator of Write On NL, Karen inspires children and adults alike to engage with the literary arts while staying curious about how the world around them is governed.
