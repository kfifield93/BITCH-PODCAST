About the Hosts

Karen Silver

Karen Silver is a children's author and journalist based in St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador. She holds a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University, where she wrote for The Muse and served as Editor-in-Chief for the 2017–2018 academic year. Karen is the creator of Write On NL, a writing program developed to bridge the gap in accessible literary and arts programming for both children and adults in Newfoundland and Labrador. She brings passion, advocacy, and storytelling to every episode of BiTransCanadaHighway.
