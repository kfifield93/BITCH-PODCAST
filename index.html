About the Podcast

BiTransCanadaHighway is an unfiltered podcast journey exploring politics, identity, culture, queerness, and everything in between — brought to you by two fierce voices from Newfoundland & Labrador.

About the Hosts

Karen Silver is a children’s author and journalist from St. John’s, NL. With a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University, she has written for The Muse and served as its Editor-in-Chief in 2017-2018. Karen is also the founder of Write On NL, a literary program for youth and adults, bridging gaps in Newfoundland and Labrador's arts scene.

Alex Noftal is co-host of the podcast and brings his own unique voice and perspective. (More coming soon!)
