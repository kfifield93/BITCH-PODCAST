About the Hosts

Karen Silver is a children's author and journalist from St. John's, NL. She has a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University of Newfoundland where she wrote for The Muse and was the Editor in Chief for the 2017-2018 academic year. Karen created Write On NL, a writing program for children and adults to fill a gap in the literary and arts fields in NL.
