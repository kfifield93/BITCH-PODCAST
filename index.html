About the Podcast

Hey, Canadians! Do you know your rights? Or are you more familiar with the rights of Americans? Media and pop culture may give us the impression that we know the laws and rights that regulate the world around us, but our understanding of the regulatory and governing bodies in Canada might be assumptions from what we know about the US. Join Karen and Alex as we sort through Canadian laws, regulations, and rights, and review real Canadian cases that reference or even influence the law in question.

About the Hosts

Karen Silver

Karen Silver is a children's author and journalist from St. John's, NL. She has a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University of Newfoundland where she wrote for the University Paper, The Muse, and was the Editor in Chief for the 2017-2018 academic year. Karen created Write On NL.
