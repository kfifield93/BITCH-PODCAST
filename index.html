About the Hosts

Karen Silver: Karen Silver is a children’s author and journalist from St. John’s, NL. She holds a BA in Communications and Linguistics from Memorial University of Newfoundland. During her time at MUN, she was Editor-in-Chief of The Muse (2017–2018) and wrote regularly for the paper. She also founded Write On NL, a writing program for both children and adults, aimed at bridging the literary gap in Newfoundland and Labrador.
